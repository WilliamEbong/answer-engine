# Some thoughts on learning

Someone did a study about how people learn things better when the practice is
spread out instead of all at once. Apparently the people who spread it out did
quite a bit better later on, and the researchers thought this was promising.
It might have been with nurses or medical students, and it took a few months.
The improvement was described as significant. There could be some downsides
but overall it seems like spacing things out is probably good for learning,
which matches what a lot of people already believe about cramming versus
studying a little at a time.
